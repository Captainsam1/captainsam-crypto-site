How It Works

Our escrow system makes P2P crypto trading simple, secure, and straightforward in just four easy steps.

1

Post Offer

Buyer or seller creates a detailed trade listing with their preferred terms.

2

Engage

Another party reviews and accepts the offer to begin the trade.

3

Escrow Activated

Crypto is securely held in our escrow system during the transaction.

4

Confirm Trade

Once both sides approve, funds are released to complete the transaction.
